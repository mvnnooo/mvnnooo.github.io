About the Artist

The Beginning: MVNNOOO was born in El Agamy, Alexandria in 1992. He grew up in the vibrant streets of Alexandria, where he was exposed to the raw energy of urban life, which later became a major influence on his music.

Musical Journey: MVNNOOO began his musical journey in 2020, crafting a distinctive style that blends Egyptian Trap, Drill, and electronic street sounds. His music is characterized by heavy rhythms, realistic storytelling, and street-inspired energy.

Musical Style:

  • Egyptian Trap with international production quality
  • Drill mixed with street narratives
  • Electronic Street Sound
  • Dark Minimal aesthetics
  • Arab Hip Hop

Collaborations: MVNNOOO has collaborated with other artists including HARLY, and continues to push the boundaries of the Egyptian urban music scene.
